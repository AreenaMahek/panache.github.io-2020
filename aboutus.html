ABOUT PANACHE

Why Marketing Website for traditional textiles ?
We live in a country  where culture differs from region to region and every region has their unique clothing style ,there are many dresses that do not belong to any brand but are beautifully designed and made of good quality by many artisans in India ,but unfortunately these artisans  remain unknown  due to lack of exposure of their work  to the world ,in order to solve this problem we are developing a marketing app that aims at setting a platform for such artists to showcase their work to large audience so that they can increase their earning. 

WANT TO KNOW MORE?

PANACHE presents to you a creative way to know about the fashion events held nearest to you along with the unknown artisans and their amazing works.
